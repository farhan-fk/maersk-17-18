# MAERSK SHIPPING - FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQ)

## SHIPPING POLICIES

### What are your shipping times?
Standard shipping times vary by route:
- Asia to North America: 14-18 days
- Europe to North America: 10-14 days
- Asia to Europe: 25-35 days
- Intra-Asia routes: 7-14 days
Express shipping options are available for time-sensitive cargo at additional cost.

### What documents do I need for international shipping?
Required documents include:
1. Commercial Invoice (3 copies minimum)
2. Packing List
3. Bill of Lading (B/L)
4. Certificate of Origin (for certain countries)
5. Export/Import licenses (if applicable)
6. Insurance certificate (recommended)

All documents must be in English or accompanied by certified translations.

### How do I track my shipment?
You can track your shipment using:
- Container number (e.g., MAEU1234567)
- Bill of Lading number
- Booking reference number
Visit our online tracking portal or contact customer service for real-time updates.

## CUSTOMS AND REGULATIONS

### What items are prohibited from shipping?
Maersk strictly prohibits:
- Weapons and ammunition
- Illegal drugs and narcotics
- Counterfeit goods
- Live animals (except with special permits)
- Radioactive materials
- Unpackaged hazardous materials

### Do you handle dangerous goods?
Yes, we transport dangerous goods (DG) classified under IMDG Code classes 1-9, provided:
- Proper documentation is submitted 7 days before shipment
- Goods are properly packed and labeled per IMDG regulations
- DG declaration form is completed by certified personnel
- Additional fees apply based on class and quantity

### What are the customs clearance requirements?
Customs clearance requires:
- Accurate commodity codes (HS codes)
- Declared value matching commercial invoice
- Payment of duties and taxes
- Compliance with destination country regulations
Processing time: 2-5 business days (varies by country)

## PRICING AND PAYMENT

### How is shipping cost calculated?
Shipping costs are based on:
1. Container type (20ft, 40ft, 40ft High Cube)
2. Weight and volume
3. Origin and destination ports
4. Freight class
5. Fuel surcharges
6. Port handling fees
7. Customs clearance fees

Request a quote online or contact our sales team for accurate pricing.

### What payment methods do you accept?
We accept:
- Wire transfer (preferred for large shipments)
- Credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, AmEx)
- Company account with credit terms (approved customers only)
- Letter of Credit (L/C) for international transactions

Payment terms: Net 30 days for approved accounts, prepayment for new customers.

## CONTAINER INFORMATION

### What container types are available?
Standard containers:
- 20ft Standard (TEU): 33.2m³ capacity, max 28,000 kg
- 40ft Standard (FEU): 67.6m³ capacity, max 28,500 kg
- 40ft High Cube: 76.3m³ capacity, max 28,600 kg

Specialized containers:
- Refrigerated containers (reefers)
- Open-top containers
- Flat rack containers
- Tank containers

### Can I share a container with other shippers?
Yes! We offer Less than Container Load (LCL) services where multiple shippers share container space.
Benefits:
- Cost-effective for smaller shipments
- Pay only for space used
- Regular weekly departures
Minimum: 1 cubic meter (CBM)

## RETURNS AND CANCELLATIONS

### What is your cancellation policy?
Cancellation terms:
- More than 7 days before shipping: Full refund minus 10% processing fee
- 3-7 days before shipping: 50% refund
- Less than 3 days: No refund (rescheduling available with fees)

For force majeure events (weather, port closures), full refund or rescheduling at no cost.

### Can I return a shipment?
Return shipments are subject to:
- Return authorization number (RAN) required
- Return shipping fees apply
- Customs clearance fees non-refundable
- Goods must be in original condition
- Perishable goods cannot be returned

Process: Contact customer service within 48 hours of delivery to initiate return.

## INSURANCE

### Is cargo insurance included?
Basic liability coverage is included (max $500 per package or $2/kg, whichever is less).
For full value coverage, we offer:
- All-Risk Marine Insurance: Covers loss, damage, theft (recommended)
- Total Loss Only: Covers complete loss of container
- Premium rates: 0.5-2% of cargo value

### What does insurance cover?
All-Risk Marine Insurance covers:
- Physical loss or damage during transit
- General average contributions
- Theft and piracy
- Weather-related damage
- Container damage

Exclusions: War, strikes, inherent vice, improper packing, delays.

## SPECIAL SERVICES

### Do you offer door-to-door delivery?
Yes! Our logistics division provides:
- Pick-up from supplier warehouse
- Customs clearance at origin and destination
- Ocean freight
- Final delivery to your warehouse
- Real-time tracking throughout

Available in 150+ countries worldwide.

### What is your temperature-controlled shipping capability?
Our refrigerated containers (reefers) maintain:
- Temperature range: -35°C to +30°C
- Humidity control available
- Continuous monitoring and alerts
- Backup power systems
- Pre-trip inspections

Ideal for: Pharmaceuticals, perishable foods, chemicals, plants.
